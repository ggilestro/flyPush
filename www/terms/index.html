5. Service Description

flyRoom is a Drosophila stock management platform. Features include:

  • Stock tracking and organisation (genotypes, sources, locations, tags)
  • Cross planning with AI-powered genotype prediction
  • FlyBase integration for searching and importing stocks from public repositories
  • Flip tracking with maintenance alerts and email reminders
  • Label printing with QR codes and barcodes
  • Collaborator sharing across labs and institutions
  • Bulk import and export (CSV/Excel)

Features may vary by subscription plan and may be updated from time to time.
