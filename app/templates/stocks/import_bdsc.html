About FlyBase Stock Import

This feature allows you to search and import stocks from multiple Drosophila stock centers via the FlyBase stock database.

Available Stock Centers:

  • BDSC - Bloomington Drosophila Stock Center
  • VDRC - Vienna Drosophila Resource Center
  • Kyoto - Kyoto Stock Center
  • NIG-Fly - NIG-Fly Stock Center
  • KDRC - Korean Drosophila Resource Center
  • FlyORF - FlyORF
  • NDSSC - National Drosophila Species Stock Center

What gets imported:

  • Stock ID: "{REPOSITORY}-{stock_number}" (e.g., BDSC-80563, VDRC-10004)
  • Genotype from FlyBase
  • Repository information
  • Links to FlyBase and original stock center pages (stored in metadata)
